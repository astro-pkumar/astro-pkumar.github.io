My research interests lie in galaxy formation and evolution, and dynamical modelling. My PhD project focuses on the evolution of the orbital distribution of galaxies from the epoch when the Universe was roughly half its current age up until today. To investigate this, I work extensively with cosmological simulations such as IllustrisTNG and high-resolution observational data from the LEGA-C survey.
